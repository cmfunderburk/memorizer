Contract Curve Derivation (Edgeworth Box)

Condition for Pareto Efficiency in pure exchange.
MRS_A = MRS_B
=== MEMO START ===
Person A: U_A(xA, yA)
Person B: U_B(xB, yB)
Endowments: xA + xB = X_bar, yA + yB = Y_bar

MRS_A = (∂U_A/∂xA) / (∂U_A/∂yA)
MRS_B = (∂U_B/∂xB) / (∂U_B/∂yB)

Pareto efficiency requires tangency of indifference curves:
MRS_A(xA, yA) = MRS_B(xB, yB)

Substitute xB = X_bar - xA, yB = Y_bar - yA into MRS_B:
MRS_A(xA, yA) = MRS_B(X_bar - xA, Y_bar - yA)

This equation defines the Contract Curve in terms of (xA, yA).
Any competitive equilibrium must lie on this curve.
